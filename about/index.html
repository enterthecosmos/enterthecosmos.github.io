Applying to PhDs is confusing.

Whether you're a high school student looking ahead or have already graduated with a Masters degree, the PhD application process is fraught with unspoken rules and mysterious terminology.

My aim is to even the playing field for underprivileged applicants and those from "non-conventional" backgrounds by explaining every step in detail. There are no pre-requisities and no hidden knowledge.

Just application advice, plainand simple.
